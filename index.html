💱 Where to exchange money

Exchange in Morocco — never before you travel. You'll get significantly better rates in Marrakech than at home or at the airport.

  • Best option — Hotel Ali Bureau de Change · Rue Moulay Ismail, near Djemaa el-Fna · Open 24/7 · Consistently rated the best rates in the city · Google Maps →
  • Bureaux de change in the medina · Several licensed offices around Djemaa el-Fna square and Guéliz · Check the rate on Google before exchanging so you know what to expect · Avoid ones near tourist hotspots
  • ATMs · Available all over Marrakech · Al Barid Bank ATMs charge no withdrawal fee · Western Union ATM near Bahia Palace is convenient · Always decline the "guaranteed exchange rate" option on screen (it's worse)
  • Airport exchange · Available 24/7 at Menara Airport arrivals · Rates are slightly lower than in the medina
  • Revolut / Wise cards · Both work well in Marrakech for ATM withdrawals at the interbank rate · Good backup option
  • Tip: Moroccan dirhams are a closed currency — you cannot exchange them back to euros easily. Don't exchange more than you need.
